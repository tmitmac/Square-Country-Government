About Square Country

Learn about our history, government structure, and national values.

Our History

Square Country was founded in 1798 as a constitutional republic based on principles of equality, justice, and innovation. Our founders envisioned a nation where all citizens would have equal opportunities to succeed.

Throughout our history, Square Country has been a leader in technological advancement and social progress. We were among the first nations to implement universal healthcare and education systems in the early 20th century.

Today, Square Country continues to be a model of good governance, with a strong economy, high standard of living, and commitment to environmental sustainability.

Government Structure

Square Country operates under a system of checks and balances between three branches of government.

Executive Branch

Headed by the Prime Minister, the executive branch implements and enforces laws. Includes the Cabinet and various executive departments.

Legislative Branch

The bicameral Parliament makes laws, controls finances, and oversees the executive. Composed of the House of Representatives and Senate.

Judicial Branch

Interprets laws and administers justice. Headed by the Supreme Court, with lower courts throughout the country.
