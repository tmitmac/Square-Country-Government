Business Registration

Find information about registering a business, licensing requirements, and official procedures in Square Country. All applications and processing are handled through the Ministry of Busines, Innovation, and Employment, and the Federal Revenue Department.

Business Application Steps

  • Determine your business type
  • Prepare supporting documents
  • Complete the official application form
  • Submit to the Ministry of Business, Innovation, and Employment

Required Documents

  • Valid passport or ID
  • Proof of address
  • Financial statements or proof of funds
  • Health and character certificates (if applicable)

Processing Times

Processing times vary depending on application type and workload. Most business applications are processed within 2–4 weeks. Expedited processing may be available for eligible applicants.

Register a Business

Important: All business applications must be completed in accordance with the Businesses Act 1983. Applications must be fully and accurately filled out; incomplete or incorrectly submitted applications may not be accepted. Please ensure all required information and documentation are provided to avoid delays.

Square Country’s Businesses Act 1983 governs the registration, operation, and regulation of all businesses. Applicants must meet all eligibility requirements, including financial, legal, and compliance obligations, before submitting a business application.

Provisional or temporary business registrations may allow entrepreneurs to operate under limited permissions while their full application is being processed. Full business registration is granted to applicants who meet all regulatory standards and demonstrate the ability to operate responsibly and legally within Square Country.
