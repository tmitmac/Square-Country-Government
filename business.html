Business Registration

Registering a business in Square Country is simple and streamlined. Use our online portal to submit your application, choose your business structure, and track the registration status.

Eligibility

  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old.
  • Must provide valid identification (passport or national ID).
  • Business name must comply with naming rules.

Required Documents

  • Completed registration form.
  • Proof of identity for all directors or partners.
  • Business address and contact information.
  • Applicable registration fees.

Processing Times

Standard business registration typically takes 5–10 business days. Expedited processing may be available for urgent registrations.
