Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Managing Square Country’s international relations, diplomacy, and foreign policy initiatives.

Ministry Overview

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs oversees Square Country’s international relations, diplomatic missions, treaties, and global partnerships.

  • Managing diplomatic missions and embassies worldwide
  • Negotiating international treaties and agreements
  • Supporting trade and foreign policy objectives
  • Providing consular services to citizens abroad

Budget Allocation by Sector

  • Diplomatic Missions: 40%
  • International Aid & Development: 25%
  • Policy & Research: 20%
  • Consular Services: 15%
