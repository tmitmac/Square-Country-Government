Driver’s Licence

Apply for, renew, or upgrade your driver’s licence in Square Country. Learn about licence types, eligibility, and the step-by-step application process.

Licence Types

  • Learner Licence – for beginners under supervision.
  • Restricted Licence – allows limited solo driving.
  • Full Licence – unrestricted driving privileges.
  • Commercial Licence – for professional or heavy vehicle drivers.

Eligibility

  • Minimum age requirements for each licence type.
  • Proof of identity and residency.
  • Passing the relevant driving theory and practical tests.

Required Documents

  • Identification (passport or birth certificate).
  • Proof of residency.
  • Completed application form.
  • Medical certificate if required (for commercial licences).

Application Steps

  1. Check which licence type you need and ensure you meet the eligibility.
  2. Gather all required documents.
  3. Complete the online application form or submit in person.
  4. Schedule and pass the theory and practical tests.
  5. Receive your driver’s licence.
