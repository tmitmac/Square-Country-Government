Driver’s Licence Services

Apply for, renew, or upgrade your driver’s licence. Learn about eligibility, licence types, and required steps in Square Country.

Licence Information

The Square Country Transport Authority oversees the issuance, renewal, and upgrade of driver’s licences for all citizens and residents.

Licence Types

  • Learner Licence – for beginners under supervision.
  • Restricted Licence – allows limited solo driving.
  • Full Licence – unrestricted driving privileges.
  • Commercial Licence – for professional or heavy vehicle drivers.

Eligibility

  • Meet minimum age for each licence class.
  • Provide proof of identity and residency.
  • Pass theory and practical driving tests.

Required Documents

  • Identification (passport or birth certificate)
  • Proof of residency
  • Completed application form
  • Medical certificate (for commercial licences)

Application Steps

  1. Confirm the licence type you need.
  2. Prepare required documentation.
  3. Submit your application online or in person.
  4. Complete and pass all required tests.
  5. Receive your official driver’s licence.
