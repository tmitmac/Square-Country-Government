Square Country Electoral Commission

Ensuring free, fair, and transparent elections for all citizens of Square Country.

Overview

The Square Country Electoral Commission (SCEC) is an independent body responsible for administering and overseeing all national and local elections. It ensures that electoral processes are conducted with integrity, transparency, and impartiality, upholding the democratic values of the nation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Administering national and local elections
  • Maintaining the national electoral roll
  • Overseeing political party and candidate registration
  • Monitoring electoral campaign finance and spending
  • Educating the public about the democratic process
  • Certifying official election results

Independence & Integrity

The Commission operates independently from the government and political parties. Its members are appointed through a transparent parliamentary process, and its funding is secured by statute. All operations are guided by the principles of fairness, transparency, and equal participation.

Recent Work

  • Introduced stricter financial reporting for political parties
  • Partnered with schools to promote youth civic engagement
