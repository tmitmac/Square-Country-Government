Ministry of Finance

Responsible for national budgeting, taxation, economic policy, and public finance management.

Ministry Overview

The Ministry of Finance manages Square Country’s national revenue, sets economic policy, and oversees public finance to ensure sustainable growth and stability. It coordinates with the central bank, revenue agencies, and international financial partners to deliver effective fiscal management.

Official Website

Visit the official Ministry of Finance website for online services, data, and policy updates.

Go to finance.squarecountry.gov

History

Established in the early 20th century, the Ministry of Finance has evolved from managing simple tax collection and national budgets to directing complex financial strategies and international economic relations. Over the years, it has been instrumental in guiding the country through economic recessions, fiscal reforms, and modernization of public finance systems.

Leadership

  • Minister of Finance: Hon. Clara Whitmore
  • Deputy Secretaries for Budget, Tax, and Economic Policy

Key Responsibilities

  • Formulating and monitoring the national budget
  • Administering taxation policy and collection
  • Managing public debt and sovereign funds
  • Providing economic analysis and forecasts
  • Coordinating financial policy with international institutions

Programs & Initiatives

  • Economic Stabilization Program – to support businesses during downturns
  • National Infrastructure Funding Initiative – allocating budgets for major projects
  • Tax Compliance & Digitalization Program – improving revenue collection efficiency
  • Green Finance Strategy – promoting sustainable investments and environmental projects

Recent Achievements

  • Balanced national budget for 3 consecutive years
  • Increased tax compliance by 15% over the last fiscal year
  • Launched digital government finance portal for public transparency
  • Funded $2B in infrastructure projects in 2024
