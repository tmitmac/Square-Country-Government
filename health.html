Health Insurance

Protect yourself and your family with Square Country’s comprehensive health insurance plans. We offer coverage for medical visits, prescriptions, hospital stays, and preventive care.

Eligibility

  • All residents of Square Country.
  • Must provide proof of identity and residency.
  • Applicants under 18 must be enrolled by a parent or guardian.

Coverage Options

  • Basic Coverage: Primary care, prescriptions, emergency care.
  • Family Plan: Covers spouses and dependents.
  • Premium Coverage: Includes specialist visits, dental, and vision care.

Enrollment Steps

  1. Gather required documents (ID, proof of residency, previous insurance info if applicable).
  2. Choose the health insurance plan that fits your needs.
  3. Complete the online enrollment form or submit your application in person.
  4. Review and confirm coverage details.
  5. Receive confirmation and insurance card.
