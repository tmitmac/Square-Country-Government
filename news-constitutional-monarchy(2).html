President speaks about last-minute decision on SC's governance.

October 23, 2025

Earlier today, President Parker released a press statement in regards to his decision to revert Square Country's electoral system to a constitutional monarchy.

"Today I have signed a key piece of legislation. The times are changing and it is clear to keep our democracy alive we must revert back to the past and use our parliamentary system," he said.

Speaking to soon-to-be Leader of the Oppostion, Hayden Pullar's, claims "this decision was not made quickly and I have been advised by many that this is the correct course of action to take."

"This means that effective immediately all government offices shall be referred to [as] ministries and the Senate will be dissolved, I shall return to being Prime Minister and my cabinet will be Ministers once more," the President announced.

"I know this decision may come as a shock but we feel it is right to ensure the people's voices are heard. Later in the week, I will be holding a press conference for any questions regarding the change and hope to see you all then. As of now we are once again a parliamentary democracy."

President Parker has chosen not to speak direclty to his counterpart, Hayden Pullar, instead, communicating through the media. Expert Robin Sinclair of the Queenstown Political Society told Square Country News that this "isn't a presidential move. It's something we would see on the school playground."

Hayden Pullar is still yet to respond.
