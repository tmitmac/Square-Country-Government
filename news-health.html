Health Insurance Reforms Announced

September 5, 2025

The Ministry of Health has introduced a set of reforms aimed at improving access to health insurance for all residents of Square Country. The reforms include updated coverage options, streamlined application processes, and subsidies for low-income families.

Under the new system, citizens will be able to choose from a wider range of insurance plans that better suit their needs, with increased transparency on benefits and costs. Digital tools have been added to simplify applications, claims, and renewals.

Officials emphasize that these reforms are designed to reduce financial barriers to healthcare and ensure that more residents have reliable access to essential medical services. The program is part of a broader initiative to strengthen the national healthcare system and improve public health outcomes across the country.
