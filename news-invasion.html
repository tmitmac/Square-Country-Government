Capital Invaded by Rivorian People's Revolutionary Army, Prime Minister eating cake in the North.

November 30, 2025

Earlier today, the People's Revolutionary Army's 7th Red Hammer Division deployed several T-72B tanks into the nation's Capital.

Almost immediately, a standoff broke out between the Rivorian Forces and the White House Security as Square tanks began their journey to Masterton.

Whilst many asked where the Prime Minister was during this time, the answer lies in his love for traditional Curwick Cakes, where he was seen enjoying the local treat with the Mexican President.

Leader-of-the-Opposition, Hayden Pullar, has strongly spoken out, dissapproving of the Prime Minister's apparent lack-of-care towards the situation. Speaking to Square Country News, Pullar exclaimed "This is what the current government calls leadership: hiding away eating cakes while his own capital is invaded by forces in a war that should have ended years ago."

More updates are to come as the standoff continues outside the White House.
