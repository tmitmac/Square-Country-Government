September 10, 2025

Square Country has launched a new scholarship program designed to support talented students pursuing higher education across the country. The initiative will provide financial assistance for tuition fees, textbooks, and living expenses.

Eligible students can apply online through the government education portal. Selection criteria include academic performance, community involvement, and financial need. Scholarships will be awarded on a competitive basis, with priority given to those demonstrating outstanding achievement and commitment.
