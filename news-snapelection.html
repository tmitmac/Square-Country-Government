Prime Minister Parker calls Snap Election

December 4, 2025

The Prime Minister spoke from the White House Press Briefing Room earlier this morning, calling a snap election.

This came after days of nationwide backlash, following the Prime Minister's woeful handling of the Rivorian Invasion, which saw serveral Rivorian tanks invade the capital city.

"While our own capital fell, the Prime Minister did nothing! Eating cakes in the north, smiling in photos with rich CEOs in the west, and refusing to answer any questions about the invasion in the east," the Leader of the Opposition said.

Parker was certain that any controversy would pass. However, the final nail in the coffin, so to speak, was the lack of faith in his leadership by his own ministers and caucus.

The election is expected to be held on December 6th, 2025.

The Freedoms Party has confirmed that the current Leader of the Oppostion will lead the party into the election. The Green Party too will retain its current leadership. It is unclear, however, if Innovation's Tina Voss will lead into the election, given her decision to step down in 2027.

The Leader of the Oppostion is still yet to respond to the election.
