Government Launches New Refreshed Website

September 15, 2025

The government has officially launched a refreshed version of its official website, aiming to provide citizens with easier access to information and services. The updated platform features a modern design, improved navigation, and a dedicated portal where users can find detailed information on all government departments. Officials say the redesign reflects a commitment to transparency and digital accessibility.

Among the key updates is the introduction of the services portal, which centralizes information on departmental functions, contact details, and available programs. Visitors can now quickly search for services, learn about departmental initiatives, and access relevant resources without navigating multiple pages. The government highlighted that the portal is mobile-friendly, ensuring that information is accessible on all devices.
