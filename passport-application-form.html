3. Passport Photograph

The photograph must be recent and clearly show the applicant’s face. Photos must be in colour, taken against a plain background, and must not include hats or facial coverings unless worn for religious reasons. Each photo will be securely stored and verified by the Department of Internal Affairs. If your photo is not acceptable, you will need to resubmit your application.

Accepted formats: JPG or PNG. Maximum file size: 5MB.
