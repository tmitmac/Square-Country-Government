Passport Application

Applying for or renewing your Square Country passport is simple and convenient. Use our online portal to submit your application, track your status, and ensure your travel documents are up-to-date.

Eligibility

  • Must be a Square Country citizen.
  • Must have a valid form of identification.
  • Previous passport (if renewing) must be available.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form.
  • Proof of citizenship (birth certificate or national ID).
  • Recent passport photos.
  • Applicable fees.

Processing Times

Standard processing takes 2–3 weeks. Expedited processing is available for urgent travel.
