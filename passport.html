Apply for a Passport

Use this service to apply for a new Square Country passport or renew an existing passport. Applications are reviewed by the Department of Internal Affairs.

Eligibility

  • You must be a Square Country citizen
  • You must provide valid identification
  • Previous passport required for renewals

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Proof of citizenship
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Application fee

Processing Time

Standard applications are processed within 2–3 weeks. Expedited services may be available for urgent travel.

Passport Application

Important: Ensure all information is correct before submitting. Providing incorrect information may delay processing.

The Passports Act 1963 states that any citizen of Square Country may apply for a passport issued by the Department of Internal Affairs for the purpose of international travel and identification. Applications may be made by individuals on their own behalf, while a parent or legal guardian may apply for a passport on behalf of a minor.

The Act also provides for the renewal, replacement, or reissue of passports, including in cases where a passport has expired, been lost, stolen, or damaged. Applicants must provide proof of citizenship, identity, and any documentation required by the issuing authority.

To complete your passport application, you must use the official online application form. Click the button below to proceed to the secure application page.
