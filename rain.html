⚠️ SEVERE WEATHER WARNING

Heavy rain and strong winds expected nationwide. Citizens are advised to take precautions immediately.
Issued: 19 December 2025 — 6:30 PM

Nationwide Emergency Weather Bulletin

The National Weather Service has issued a severe weather warning for the Northern Teritorries west of the Prunty Ranges. Forecasts indicate prolonged heavy rainfall accompanied by persistant thunderstorms, and strong to gale-force winds.

Emergency services are on standby. Members of the public are advised to:

  • Avoid unnecessary travel
  • Secure loose outdoor items
  • Monitor local radio and television broadcasts
  • Follow instructions from emergency authorities
  • Always be prepared to evacuate

Schools and public services will most likely be affected. Further updates will be issued as information becomes available.

The Prime Minister and Director-General of the Civil Defence and Emergency Management Agency are expected to hold a press-conference at around 8:00PM. This will be available to listen to on all radio frequencies and television channels.

Always remember: Be strong, be smart, be safe.
