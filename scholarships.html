Scholarships

Square Country offers a variety of scholarships to support students in their educational journey. Explore available scholarships, eligibility criteria, and how to apply.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident or citizen of Square Country.
  • Academic or extracurricular excellence as required by the specific scholarship.
  • Must be enrolled or planning to enroll in a recognized educational institution.

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts or report cards.
  • Personal statement or essay.
  • Letters of recommendation (if required).
  • Proof of enrollment or acceptance letter.

How to Apply

  1. Review scholarship options and eligibility.
  2. Gather required documents and prepare your application.
  3. Complete the online application form or submit documents to the relevant office.
  4. Submit your application before the stated deadline.
  5. Wait for notification of results.
