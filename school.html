School Enrollment

Enroll your child in Square Country schools with ease. Our platform guides parents through eligibility, documentation, and the step-by-step enrollment process.

Eligibility

  • Children must be of school-going age (typically 5–18 years).
  • Proof of residency in Square Country is required.
  • Previous school records for transfer students.

Required Documents

  • Child’s birth certificate or passport.
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, lease, or official letter).
  • Previous school transcripts or report cards (if applicable).
  • Vaccination/health records.

Enrollment Steps

  1. Gather all required documents.
  2. Complete the online enrollment form or submit documents in person.
  3. Schedule an interview or assessment if required.
  4. Receive enrollment confirmation and school assignment.
  5. Prepare for the first day of school.
