Tax Filing

Filing your taxes in Square Country is simple and transparent. Whether you're an individual or a business, our platform guides you through the process, ensuring compliance and avoiding penalties.

Who Needs to File

  • All citizens with taxable income.
  • Businesses operating in Square Country.
  • Foreign residents earning income locally.

Required Documents

  • Income statements (employment, business, investments).
  • Receipts for deductible expenses.
  • Previous year’s tax returns (if applicable).
  • Any official correspondence from the tax authority.

How to File

  1. Gather all required documents and information.
  2. Log in to the official Square Country tax portal.
  3. Complete the online forms following the guided instructions.
  4. Review your entries carefully to avoid errors.
  5. Submit your tax filing electronically before the deadline.
