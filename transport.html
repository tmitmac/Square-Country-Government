Public Transport Pass

Get your Square Country public transport pass to access buses, trains, and trams efficiently. Explore pass types, eligibility, and how to apply.

Pass Types

  • Student Pass – discounted fare for full-time students.
  • Adult Pass – standard fare for regular commuters.
  • Senior / Concession Pass – reduced fare for seniors and eligible residents.
  • Tourist / Visitor Pass – short-term passes for visitors.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Square Country (except for tourist passes).
  • Proof of age or student status may be required.
  • Valid identification for verification.

How to Apply

  1. Select the type of pass that suits you.
  2. Gather required documents (ID, proof of residence or student status).
  3. Complete the online application form or apply at a service center.
  4. Pay the applicable fee if required.
  5. Receive your pass and enjoy public transport.
