Immigration and Visas

Find information about visas, residency, and immigration requirements for entering or staying in Square Country. All applications and official processing are completed through the Ministry of Immigration website.

Visa Categories

  • Visitor visas
  • Work visas
  • Student visas
  • Residence visas

Basic Requirements

  • Valid passport
  • Proof of funds (if required)
  • Health and character checks
  • Supporting documentation

Processing Times

Processing times vary depending on visa type and demand. Most temporary visas are processed within 2–6 weeks.

Apply for a Visa

Important: Applications must be submitted through the official Ministry of Immigration website. Incomplete or incorrect applications may be delayed or declined.

Square Country’s Immigration Act provides for the entry, stay, and residence of non-citizens. Applicants must meet all eligibility criteria relevant to their visa category, including financial, health, and character requirements.

Temporary visas allow individuals to visit, study, or work in Square Country for a limited period. Residence visas may be granted to individuals with skills, employment, or family connections that contribute to the country.

Visa applications can no longer be filed through the general government services site. Instead, they can be accessed through the Ministry of Immigration website. To begin your application, check your eligibility, or track an existing application, use the official Ministry of Immigration service portal.
