Visa Services

Square Country offers a variety of visas for tourists, business travelers, students, and work purposes. You can check eligibility, submit applications, and track the status of your visa online.

Types of Visas

  • Tourist Visa
  • Business Visa
  • Student Visa
  • Work Visa
  • Diplomatic Visa

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity.
  • Completed visa application form.
  • Proof of financial support or sponsorship.
  • Additional documents depending on visa type (e.g., invitation letter, admission letter for students).

Processing Times

Processing times vary by visa type. Tourist visas typically take 5–10 business days, while work or student visas may take longer.
