Vote in Square Country

To successfully submit your vote, you must provide all of the following:

  • Full Name
  • State of Registry
  • Citizenship Number
  • Candidate Choice

Note: Voting is no longer accessed through the Square Country Government Website. Instead, you can find all information on voting, and voting itself, on the Square Country Electoral Website.
