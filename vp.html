Deputy Prime Minister Layton Cox

Second-in-command of Square Country, assisting in governance, national development, and policy implementation.

Overview

Deputy Prime Minister Layton Cox supports the Prime Minister in executing national policies, representing Square Country in official matters, and overseeing key government initiatives. He focuses on education, social programs, and economic development.

Career & History

Layton Cox previously served as Leader of the United Mexican States and held various leadership positions. His experience emphasizes educational reform, social equity, and community development. He has been instrumental in drafting policies that strengthen governance and public engagement.

Leadership Role

  • Assists the Prime Minister in policy implementation
  • Oversees Education and Social Development initiatives
  • Represents Square Country at domestic and international events

Key Policies

  • Education reform and workforce development
  • Strengthening social programs and equity initiatives
  • Support for economic and regional development projects
  • Enhancing public sector transparency and governance

Programs & Initiatives

  • National Education Excellence Program – improving schools and training
  • Community Development & Social Welfare Initiative – supporting local projects
  • Workforce Training & Innovation Fund – modernizing skills across sectors
  • Representation & Diplomacy Program – acting on behalf of Square Country

Recent Achievements

  • Successfully launched nationwide educational reform programs
  • Implemented community and social development initiatives
  • Strengthened regional economic development projects
  • Represented Square Country at key international forums
